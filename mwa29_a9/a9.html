Interactive Computer Graphics - Assignment 9

Texture Mapping


Topics:

  • 2D and 3D Texture Mapping

  • Procedural Texture Mapping



This assignment involves writing two programs.
  1. In the first program you will extend your HW7 program by placing an image-based texture map on the Bezier patch.
    • You may read in an image, or you can procedurally generate the image data.
    • The 2D texture should be applied to the patch in the fragment shader.
  2. The second program will extend your HW6 program with 3D procedural texturing.
    • Calculate an "interesting" (not just a checkerboard or simple stripes) 3D texture that is applied to an smf model in your fragment shader.
    • Incorporate the texture data into the Phong shading model.

Grading Scheme

  1. 2D image-based texture mapping: 4 points
  2. All HW7 features present: 1 point
  3. 3D procedural texture mapping: 4 points
  4. All HW6 features present: 1 point
